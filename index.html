About Us

Event Manager is designed to simplify the process of managing and booking events. Whether you need to organize a corporate gathering, wedding, or any other event, our platform provides the tools you need to ensure a smooth and successful experience.

"It’s All In The Details!" – My Motto

EventManager is a full-service event and wedding planning company located in Oregon, Operating PNW & Thailand.

We offer professional event planning and coordination service for intimate weddings, corporate events, gala dinner & more.

Enhanced with more than a decade of local and international experience, our network of supplier contacts, built over a lifetime, allows us to negotiate the best possible deal for every aspect of your wedding. We will assist in keeping you organized, and ensure that not even the smallest details get overlooked, offering a truly tailored approach to planning your wedding & events.
